Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Algorithm behavior tracking work? ?
AlgoTrack uses advanced Algorithm models (rule engine minimax locally) to process every user interaction � hovers, scrolls, clicks, and mouse movements. It detects patterns and intent in real-time, then triggers personalized responses.
Is my customer data secure? ?
Absolutely. All behavior data is processed locally on your server using our rule engine AI. No data leaves your infrastructure. We never store personal identifiable information in the behavior logs.
How are discounts calculated? ?
The Algorithm evaluates multiple factors: how long a user hovers on pricing, how many times they scroll through services, their exit intent signals, and overall engagement patterns. It then suggests an optimal discount (0-30%) tailored to that user.
Can I customize the toast messages? ?
Yes! You can customize the message templates, colors, positioning, and timing in the admin dashboard. The Algorithm will use your templates while dynamically filling in the service name and discount percentage.
What types of businesses benefit most? ?
Any business with multiple services or products benefits tremendously. E-commerce stores, SaaS platforms, digital agencies, and service-based businesses see the highest conversion improvements (averaging 30-50% lift).
